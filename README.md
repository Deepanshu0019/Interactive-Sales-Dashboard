# Interactive-Sales-Dashboard-using-excel
An interactive Excel dashboard showcasing Adidas US sales performance with dynamic filters for year, region, and product. Visualize monthly revenue trends, retailer contributions, regional sales distribution, and sales methods through charts and maps for quick, data-driven insights.

Features

Sales Trend (Line Chart): Monthly sales performance in USD for selected years.
Sales by Retailers (Bar Chart): Compare top retailers like Walmart, Amazon, Foot Locker, and more.
Sales by Region (Map): Visual distribution of sales across US regions.
Sales Method (Donut Chart): Breakdown of In-store, Online, and Outlet sales.

Interactive Filters:

Year (2020, 2021)
Region (Midwest, Northeast, South, Southeast, West)
Product Categories (Men’s & Women’s Apparel and Footwear)

Requirements

Microsoft Excel 2019 or later (recommended for full interactivity)
Enable editing and macros (if prompted)

Data Insights You Can Get

Identify peak sales months
Find top-performing retailers
Analyze which regions drive the most revenue
Understand preferred customer buying methods
